Hidden oases

where work, coffee & nature intertwine

These three spots are more than just cafés; they are hidden getaways right in the heart of Aarhus!

Plukk, LYNfabrikken & Domen might not ring a bell, but they are the secret ingredient for a perfect blend of work, coffee & nature.

In a city where go-to coffee places are to be found on every corner, these lesser-known spots are like hidden treasures, offering a feeling of escapism from the city hustle.

Plukk

Wanna get lost in the city and become one with nature?

Plukk offers a unique combination of a café, workspace & a plant shop.

The place looks and feels like a botanical garden, making it the perfect spot for a getaway from the routine in the city and reconnection with nature.

LYNfabrikken

Do you need a hideaway with a nice view?

Tucked away in a former factory building located in a charming backyard in the middle of Aarhus, LYNfabrikken sets the scene for work or study group meetings, informal conversations and exceptionally good coffee!

A cool bonus - it has the best rooftop terrace!

Domen

Do you want to have the sea at your fingertips?

Even though its location is right in the centre of Aarhus, Domen is referred as to “the hidden pearl by the sea”. It is a cosy oasis in the middle of the city, combining plants, earth colors, and tasty coffee.

Experience Domen: where urban energy meets coastal calm!
