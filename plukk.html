Café
An ideal place for relaxation, informal conversations and brunch & coffee with friends!

Workspace
With 35 available seats and a meeting room with capacity up to 40 people, called “the Greenhouse”, Plukk can also be used as a co-working space for work and study meetings.

Plant shop
At Plukk, we create adventurous terrariums. Using photosynthesis, they form their own ecosystem and take care of themselves. You can either order hand-crafted terrariums that are ready-made by us, or you can buy a DIY kit where you create the landscape of your terrarium yourself.
