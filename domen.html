NOT work/ Study friendly     •     Coffee & Brunch     •     View to the sea

• •

Café
An ideal place for relaxation, informal conversations and brunch & coffee with friends!

Domen is referred as to “the hidden pearl by the sea”. It is a cosy oasis in the middle of the city, combining plants, earth colors, and tasty coffee. There are a lot of sofas and lounge furniture, where one can sit back and relax. It is a shared space, intended to create strong connections and community.
The place is built all around the concept of sustainability - from cross-laminated solid wood without the use of nails and hammers.
